Here are some of the best student rated places to study on campus!

Be sure to give these places a try if you're trying to study!

First Favorite Spot Amongst Students:


UMBC Library


Highlights From Students: Great for quiet studying, group planning; Maximum comfort when studying; Little disruption going on.

Second Favorite Spot Amongst Students:


UMBC Commons!

Highlights From Students: Great place to study if you want to eat; Cafe like environment; High traffic area, a lot of different noises

Third Favorite Spot Amongst Students:


UMBC Interdisciplinary Life Sciences Building

Highlights: Newest building; Great views; Multiple individual study rooms; Moderate traffic depending on hour.
